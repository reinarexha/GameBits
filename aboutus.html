Frequently Asked Questions

What is Gamebits?

Gamebits is a collection of short, skill-focused leadership mini-games. Each round takes 1–3 minutes and targets a specific behavior like delegation or communication.

How do scores and the leaderboard work?

Your best score per game is saved locally in your browser and appears on the global leaderboard page on your device. You can reset scores anytime.

Is Gamebits for teams or individuals?

Both! Individuals can practice solo. Teams often use Gamebits to spark discussion in workshops, retros, and onboarding.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required. Everything runs in your browser. If we add cloud profiles later, we’ll keep you posted on the About page.

What skills do the mini-games cover?

Delegation, conflict resolution, time management, decision-making under pressure, focus & prioritization, and clear communication.
